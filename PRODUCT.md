# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users
- **Restaurant Guests / Diners**: High-end dining customers scanning QR codes at tables or using self-service. They expect an ultra-premium, smooth, and high-fidelity menu, ordering, and feedback experience.
- **Restaurant Operators / Admins / Franchise Owners**: Internal management users tracking real-time orders (KDS), customer profiles, engagement analytics, and marketing templates via the CMS. They need high clarity, speed, and usability.

## Product Purpose
Napkiq is an end-to-end, ultra-premium restaurant management, dining, customer engagement, and order orchestration platform designed for high-end dining experiences. It combines a buttery-smooth, interactive customer-facing web app with a robust, role-aware internal Content Management System (CMS) and an automated engagement engine.

## Positioning
Napkiq delivers a buttery-smooth, ultra-premium digital dining experience that bridges diners and operators with flawless design and smart automation.

## Brand Personality
Warm luxury, ultra-premium, elegant, buttery-smooth.

## Anti-references
- Standard template-based, low-contrast SaaS dashboards.
- Slow, cluttered online ordering menus with jumpy animations.
- Saturated default cream/beige AI templates.

## Design Principles
- **Sensory Premiumness**: Every element—from color transitions to custom SVGs—must feel handcrafted, responsive, and luxury-grade.
- **Usable Restraint**: Focus on readability, clean layouts, and functional animations over decorative clutter.
- **Operational Clarity**: High accessibility, clear states, and zero friction for both diners in a rush and staff managing a kitchen.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Target WCAG 2.1 AA compliance with high-contrast text and interactive elements.
- Accessible touch target sizes on mobile screens (minimum 44x44px).
- Proper support for reduced motion preferences (`prefers-reduced-motion`).
